Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

PinTrend Analytics Portal ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data.

1. Data Collection

We do not store or sell any personal user information. Our App only accesses Pinterest Analytics data (impressions, clicks, saves) through official API or authorized scrapers to provide visualization for creators.

2. Data Usage

The data retrieved is used solely for generating the performance charts and keyword research results displayed on your dashboard. We do not share this data with third parties.

3. Security

All data processing is handled securely. We encourage users to use official OAuth 2.0 authentication for the most secure experience.
